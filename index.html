About Me

Front-End Developer from Medicine Hat

From a young age I knew I'd end up with a career involving computers. At the very least I knew I'd be using computers. At the very least I'd be spending a lot of time with them. I still remember my Comm Tech teacher introducing programming through Game Maker. We used the drop-n-drag functions to build our first maze game. I enjoyed it so much that I spent the next 6 months of free time messing around with the built in Game Maker Language. That's when I decided to go into Software development.

Despite my enjoyment of programming, I didn't end up going straight into it. I spent some time pursuing teaching, engineering, and most recently, getting my pilots licence.
